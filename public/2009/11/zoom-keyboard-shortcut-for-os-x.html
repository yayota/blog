Tip: Zoom keyboard shortcut for OS X

In the Terminal run:

defaults write NSGlobalDomain NSUserKeyEquivalents '{"Zoom" = "@^Z"; "Zoom Window" = "@^Z"; }'

Quit and relaunch your applications, and ⌃⌘Z should zoom and unzoom.
